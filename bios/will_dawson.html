Will Dawson

Vice President

Will is one of the top client strategists at Ragnar Research. Prior to joining Ragnar Research, Will served as the Director of Data Operations for In Field Strategies where he was pivotal in building data-driven field programs for top political organizations, such as the Republican National Committee and the Congressional Leadership Fund, to county election candidates. Will has a natural curiosity for utilizing data to help campaigns develop effective strategies to reach the right voters with the right message. He’s a fervent believer that less can be more when it comes to political data.

His dedication to a client’s success thrives on a rigorous pursuit for quality, plus many late nights living off coffee and pizza throughout an election cycle. When he’s not working, Will can be found watching anime, hanging out with his cat Charlie, or enjoying twenty-five cent wing night with a few good buddies.
