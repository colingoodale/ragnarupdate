Chelsea Goodale

Partner

Chelsea manages research design and analysis. She has extensive experience executing quantitative and qualitative research for corporate and political clients. She has a particularly obsessive interest in research designed to help clients develop their brand and craft their narrative.

Chelsea is a tireless champion of quality reviews, a passionate advocate for data visualization, and always up for a research challenge. When she’s not working late into the night to uncover the answers our clients need, she’s probably out hiking or playing fetch with her two very young, very active dogs.
